STAGE 1 (ONSITE) External Penetration Testing (Black Box) - 700 Public IPs Cytomate's external vulnerability assessment and penetration testing methodology evaluates MOPH's security posture leveraging PTES and OWASP frameworks. Our comprehensive assessment encompasses full attack simulation cycles from reconnaissance to exploitation across all specified internet-facing infrastructure components, including black-box testing of all internet-facing services to identify exploitable vulnerabilities and weak configurations using objective-based penetration testing methods, website scraping, and defacement. Our assessment will execute complete attack chains across MOPH's external network infrastructure utilizing Cytomate's Breach+ platform for advanced attack simulation and emulation. The engagement will progress through reconnaissance, enumeration, vulnerability scanning, and exploitation. Testing includes evaluation of perimeter security controls, identification of exposed services, and validation of network segmentation controls. Cytomate's assessment will implement multiple Tactics, Techniques, and Procedures (TTPs) to simulate real-world adversary behavior and validate security controls effectiveness across the infrastructure. All testing activities will be mapped to the MITRE ATT&CK framework by Cytomate to ensure comprehensive coverage of attack vectors and provide structured threat intelligence for defensive improvements. All testing will be performed onsite at MOPH premises using locally available resources with no remote delivery. Deliverables include detailed vulnerability reports with remediation steps, dashboard and portal access to management platform, integration with risk management platform, and training sessions for team members.
